Introduction

What is Plexus AI?

Plexus AI is a hospital-embedded Centre of Excellence (CoE) focused on the real-world validation and deployment of healthcare AI and MedTech solutions.

The objective is to create a structured ecosystem where innovation is not only developed, but meaningfully tested, validated, and integrated into live clinical workflows.

The Problem

Why AI fails at the last mile

Despite rapid advancements, adoption of healthcare AI in real clinical practice remains severely limited. Promising technologies fail to translate into patient care.

  • Lack of real-world clinical validation
  • Limited doctor involvement in development
  • Poor integration into hospital workflows
  • Absence of structured real-world evidence (RWE)
The gap is not in innovation — it's in translation. No structured pathway exists in India for an AI tool to be tested in a real hospital, earn clinical evidence, and gain the trust of regulators, clinicians, and investors — simultaneously.
Our Approach

From lab to live wards

Plexus AI bridges this gap by operating directly within a functioning hospital ecosystem.

  • Deployment of AI tools in real clinical environments
  • Active clinician participation in evaluation and feedback
  • Continuous data capture on usage, outcomes, efficiency
  • Structured validation and reporting for scale-up
