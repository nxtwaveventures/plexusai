The Problem

Healthcare AI is failing at the last mile

India generates 1.4 billion patient interactions annually, yet fewer than 3% of healthcare AI tools deployed in India have undergone any structured clinical validation. The result: tools built in labs that collapse inside real wards.

"There is no structured pathway in India for an AI startup to test their product inside a real hospital, gather clinical evidence, and earn the trust of regulators, investors, and clinicians — all at once."

AI companies spend 2–4 years attempting to access hospitals independently. They face fragmented data, ethical approval bottlenecks, clinician distrust, and regulatory opacity — with no neutral intermediary to bridge the gap.

The Solution

A hospital-embedded AI innovation hub

PlexusAI is the neutral infrastructure layer between AI builders and Indian hospitals. We provide structured access to real clinical environments, issue market-trusted validation certificates, and accelerate the highest-potential startups through equity-based co-development.

Think of us as the FDA-equivalent confidence layer — but built for speed, for India, and for the current state of healthcare AI adoption.
